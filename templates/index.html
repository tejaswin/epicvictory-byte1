What are the housing location preferences for households with a high income in the city of Pittsburgh?

For standardization, we have set the income threshold of families at $100,000.


As we observe, households with income higher than $100,000 generally prefer to live in the suburbs, and away from the center of the city.
The green points show households with income over $100,000, while the red points show households with income below $100,000.
